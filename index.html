Welcome to Hawthorne Station!

Our community offers 73 beautiful homes just minutes from the Canyon Park Corridor, City of Mill Creek, and within easy commute of Seattle or the Eastside. We are located in the outstanding Northshore school district, within walking distance to Canyon Creek Elementary and Skyview Jr High.

We encourage homeowners to explore our website, whether you are a current or future member of our community. There are opportunities to get involved as Board Members or assist with various special projects.

For any questions about Hawthorne Station, please feel free to contact us by email at HOA@Hawthornestation.com.
