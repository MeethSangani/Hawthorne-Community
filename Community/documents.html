Governing Documents Overview

The Association’s economic well‐being relies on the timely payment of assessments and other allowable charges. As it is the Board's responsibility, as defined in the By‐Laws of the Association and the Declarations, to use its best efforts to collect funds owed to the Association, the listed policies and procedures have been adopted by the Board of Directors of the Hawthorne Station Homeowners’ Association.

Rules & Regulations

These Rules and Regulations have been established in the best interest of the Association as a whole. They are enforced by the Board of Directors or its designated agents in a fair and consistent manner. These rules apply to every Lot Owner and their family, guests, invitees, and licensees, ensuring a peaceful and orderly community.
